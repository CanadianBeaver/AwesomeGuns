Aircrafts, launchers and cannons

The dogfights in the OpneXcom game are not well balanced. Interceptors that are equipped with six Avalanche missiles can easily defeat the UFOs, except the Battleships. Three or four interceptors can take down any UFO without problems. It means that interceptors and missile launchers are too good in the beginning of OpenXcom game. The Awesome Guns modification changes this situation. Interceptors are weak, conventional launchers have low accuracy.
