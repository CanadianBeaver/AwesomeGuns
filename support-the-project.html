Support the Project!

Awesome Guns modification is a project powered by a very small team and depends on people volunteering time, money, and enthusiastic to keep it going. This page will help you if you are interested in supporting the project in any way.

Share your ideas, testing and bug fixing

Awesome Guns modification is constantly evolving. It would be great if you want to share your interesting ideas that can be implemented. Also, if you notice any mistakes or omissions during the game, please let us know.

Translations

Awesome Guns modification is a multilingual project. English, Russian and some Polish articles have been implemented. You can help to translate Awesome Guns modification into other languages. In addition, you can improve the current articles in English. Please, visit the translation page for more details.

Donating

Awesome Guns modification is free, but there are a lot of things that require a lot of money. Software development and testing, drawing animation and purchasing the equipment. If you would like to support it, please make a donation via PayPal. This money will be used for research, development and ongoing expenses.

Linking and recommending

Awesome Guns modification lives mostly through the attention of many active and passive fans. You can help increase the attention of our audience by talking about the project to your friends and acquaintances.

Moreover, if you have your own website then you can publish a link to the Awesome Guns modification on your website.

You can recommend Awesome Guns modification on the OpenXcom board.
